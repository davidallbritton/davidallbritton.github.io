Research Projects:
General Comments
• Double-space everything
• Write out your coding scheme (content analysis protocol) and put it in
an appendix
• For internet ads, give URL and exactly what search terms were used,
how many ads were returned, and how you selected from among them.
• Coding guide vs. Content Analysis Protocol (coding scheme)
• Appendices: start with A, label each indicating its content
• Manifest content coding:  Be careful of biasing your results by which
words you code for, particularly if your IV is gender
• DV coding:  If you are coding a yes/no variable (such as whether ads
say they are seeking an attractive partner), use 0=no and 1=yes
• Make sure your IV varies – it must have 2 or more levels.
