Reporting the Results
•Report the value of chi-square, the degrees of freedom (df), and the p value.  Also mention how many observations there were. 
•EXAMPLE:  “A greater proportion of men than women mentioned footware in their ads (see Table 1).  Of the six ads placed by men, 83% mentioned footware.  Only 17% of the six ads placed by women mentioned footware.  This difference was significant by a Chi-square test, Chi-square (1) = 5.3, p < .05.”
