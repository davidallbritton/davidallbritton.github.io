Validity of Research
• Internal Validity
• Does the IV (and not some other factor) really cause the
changes in the DV?
• Ruling out other causes (confounds)
• Construct Validity
• Does the study support the theory it is supposed to and no
other theories?
• Ruling out other theories (alternative explanations)
• External Validity
• Do the results generalize to other people?
• Statistical Validity
• Does the IV cause the changes in the DV, or could they be due
to chance?
