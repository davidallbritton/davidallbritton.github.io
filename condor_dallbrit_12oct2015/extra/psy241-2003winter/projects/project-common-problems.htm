Subject: 241 projects

 

 

General comments:

 

1.  You must compare 2 things and predict a difference.

 - Can not predict that both men and women will do the same thing (proving

   the null hypothesis)

 - Can not make a prediction about just one group ("women will seek rich

   men").  Without a standard of comparison, how will you know how to

   interpret the results?

 

2.  IV will typically be gender, and should probably have 2 levels.

 

3.  DV could be either categorical ("whether the ad... yes/no") or

    quantitative ("how many times they mention x in the ad").

  - The *same* DV must be measured for both levels of the IV (for men

    and women, for example).

 

4.  The operational definition of your DV consists of the rules you will

    use to decide how to code a value (such as yes/no) for each ad.  So

    your content analysis protocol spells out your operational definition

    of the DV in detail.

 

5.  Follow APA style in your heading formats.  Most of you will have 2

    levels of headings:

 

                            Method

    Participants

    Design

    Materials

    Procedure

                            Results

                           Discussion

                           References

                           Appendix A

                           Appendix B

                           Appendix C

                             Table 1

 

  Use level 1 and level 3 headings.  Level 1 is centered with title-case

  capitalization and no underlining.  Level 3 is flush left, title-case

  capitalization, underlined, on a line by itself (not part of a

  paragraph).

 

6.  Selecting ads.

- Do NOT select only ads that fit your hypothesis!  You

  must not select ads based on values of your dependent variables!

- You can select ads randomly using a random number table, or just take

  the first 30 ads you find.  If there is no reason to think the order of

  the ads is systematic or biased, taking the first 30 (or however many)

  is ok.

- If you restrict your sample based on some selection variable (such as

  only heterosexual ads) you must explain why.
