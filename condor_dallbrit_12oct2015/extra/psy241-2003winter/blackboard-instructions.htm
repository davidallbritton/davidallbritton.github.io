Instructions for creating blackboard account
  • Go to: www.blackboard.depaul.edu
  • Click on "create account"
  • Enter information, click "create new account" at bottom
  • you will need to remember the username and password you enter
  • in the "email address" field, be sure to put the address where you want to receive mail. That is the addess I will use if I send out an announcement to the class.
  • Click "login" on the left of the screen
  • When prompted, enter the user name and password you just defined.
  • Click on "courses"
  • Click on "couse catalog"
  • Scroll down to Psy 241 and click on it
  • Click on "enroll in this course" at the lower left of the screen
