In this lab session, you will participate in a Coglab online experiment and report your data using Blackboard.  You will also complete a Blackboard quiz in which you answer questions about the experiment and, in the process, review concepts that will be on the midterm.

 

Lab 2 Assignment:

 

  1. Launch Netscape or Internet Explorer and go to my home page (http://condor.depaul.edu/~dallbrit)
  2. Go to Coglab by clicking on the link on my home page.
  3. In the row for the topic "Language," click on " Word Superiority."
  4. Read the instructions and then log in as guest.
  5. Click "Click here to start" to begin the experiment.  Remember that the procedure is as follows:
    1. Press the spacebar to begin a trial.
    2. Stare at the fixation point when it appears.
    3. Try to see what the letters are that flash quickly on the screen before the masking stimulus appears.
    4. Press the appropriate letter on the keyboard to indicate which letter you think was in the indicated location.  Guess if you do not know.
    5. Press the spacebar again to start the next trial.
  6. At the end of the experiment, your means will appear on the screen.  Write them down here:

_________ word trials

_________ isolated letter trials
Be sure to write them down when they appear!  Once you close that window your data is gone and can not be recovered.

  2. Take the Quiz entitled "Quiz for Lab Session 2" and submit your answers in Blackboard.
