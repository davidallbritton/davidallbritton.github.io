Lab 2.14 - Reading and Inferences

Instructions for lab report

 

The lab report should be a complete APA style paper for the experiment you did in the lab.  Include the table and figure from the MEL workbook as Table 1 and Figure 1 (etc.) in your lab report. 

 

The DV for this assignment should be reading time for the target sentences (rt).  Be sure to state in your introduction what the predictions were (based on previous research on these types of inferences).  For each prediction, state whether it was confirmed, and present the statistics that support that statement.  Also report all other main effects and interactions and whether they were significant.

 

Also report the results using accuracy on the contradiction detection task (ac) as the DV.  The purpose for analyzing the accuracy data is just to make sure that there are no speed-accuracy trade-offs that would undermine your explanation of the reading time data.  You do not have to specify what the predictions were for the accuracy analysis; just note whether there were any main effects and interactions, and if there are, note whether they indicate a speed-accuracy trade-off.
