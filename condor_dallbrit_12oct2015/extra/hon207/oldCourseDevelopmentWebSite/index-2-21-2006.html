Honors 207, Cognitive Science

This web site is the temporary home of the new course in cognitive science being developed for the DePaul Honors Program. The course is tentatively titled "Honors 207, Mind, Computers, and Human Behavior." But that could change.

The purpose of this web site (and its eventual successor site in a more permanant location) is to collect notes, activities, syllabi, and any other materials that might be useful to instructors of Hon 207 and make them easily available in one place. For now, all materials are publicly accessible, but at some point we may add a password-protected subdirectory for instructors only.

If you have any programs, notes, outlines, activities, or other materials to add to this repository, please email them to David Allbritton, dallbrit@depaul.edu
