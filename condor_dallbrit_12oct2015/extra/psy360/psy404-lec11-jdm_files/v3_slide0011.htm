Sunk Costs
• Rationally, we should not consider sunk
(unrecoverable) costs when deciding what to do
next.  But we do.
– Would you walk out of a boring movie?
– What if admission had been free?
– What if you had paid $50 to see it?
• Should you sell your stocks if you think they will
go down in price?  What if you paid twice the
current price when you bought it?
