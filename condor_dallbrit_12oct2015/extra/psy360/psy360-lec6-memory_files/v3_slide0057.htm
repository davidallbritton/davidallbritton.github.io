Encoding in STM
• Primarily Auditory / Phonological
– Sound-based errors in recall of visually presented
letters (Conrad, 1964)
– More words can be recalled if they are short (fast to be
pronounced) (Baddeley, Thomson, & Buchanan,1975)
• Visual
– Letter matching:  AA faster than Aa with ISI of 2
seconds or less (Posner & Keele, 1967)
• Semantic
– Release from Proactive Interference (Wickens, 1970)
