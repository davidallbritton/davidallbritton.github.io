  • ALEKS (http://www.aleks.com) is a commercial math tutoring program for use over the web. If you need extra practice with percentages, algebra, etc., you can sign up for the free trial and try the "Foundations of Business Math" module under "Adult and Continuing Professional Education: Business." If you want to use it beyond the free trial period, it is about $20 per month.
