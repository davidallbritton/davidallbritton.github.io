Creating a national homicide profile using data from the Statistical Abstract.

** You may work with other people on this, but you must turn in your own copy and your descriptions should be your own. If you did work with other people please acknowledge that at the top of your paper.

Through this assignment you will not only have the chance to practice data analysis and graphical representation, but you will get a thorough introduction to the United States Statistical Abstract. This is a huge data base of statistics describing different aspects of our society (population, economy, crime, standard of living, etc.).

In this activity,  you will recreate most of the charts from the first article in the Chicago Tribune homicide series. Instead of focusing on Chicago, however, you will use data from the Statistical Abstract to create a national profile of homicide offenders and victims. By working on this project you will gain a behind-the-scenes look at how these charts were produced; you will be able to draw an interesting comparison between Chicago and the entire U.S.; and finally you will learn all of the necessary skills for your final group project.

Record all of your answers in a Word document. You may find the following guide to be a helpful resource for this assignment:  Guide to the Statistical Abstract.
