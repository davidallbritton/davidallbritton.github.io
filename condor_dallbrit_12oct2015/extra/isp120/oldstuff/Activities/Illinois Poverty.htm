Activity: Illinois Poverty

Open the file entitled PovertyIL.xls. This file contains 1990 population figures for each county in Illinois along with the percentage of this population living below the poverty level. Take a minute to familiarize yourself with the data.

a) Using the Thematic Mapping Tool, make a shaded map of the poverty rate (column C).  Paste your map into your Word document and then in a well-written paragraph describe the geographic patterns and note outliers.

b) Sort the counties by percentage of the population below the poverty level. What are the three counties having the highest percentage of population below the poverty level?

c) On the map of Illinois counties, locate the three counties with the highest percentage of population below the poverty level.  (The easiest way to do this is to make a map of just the three counties.  Put their names in both columns of the Thematic Mapping Tool and make a categorical map.) Describe their geographic location.

d) In column D, calculate, for each county, the number of individuals below the poverty level. Now sort the counties on column D. What are the three counties with the highest number of individuals below the poverty level?

e) Again locate the counties on the map. In a well written sentence describe the location of the counties with the largest number of people living under the poverty level. Each of these counties is a part of a large metropolitan area.  Which metropolitan areas are they?

f) What percentage of all Illinois residents below the poverty level lives in Cook County? in Alexander County? (To answer this question, you will need to find the sum of the column you added in question 4d.  The easiest way to do so is to highlight the column you want to sum and click on the sum button .  Excel will automatically put the sum at the bottom of the column.)

g) Suppose you are in the Illinois State Legislature and there are $2 million dollars earmarked to help the poor in Illinois. If the money were distributed to each county in proportion to the number of individuals below the poverty level, how much would be apportioned to Cook County? To Alexander County?

h) In a short paragraph write a convincing argument that the counties with the highest percentage of individuals below the poverty level are most in need.  Incorporate results of your analysis above into your argument.

i) In a short paragraph write a convincing argument that the counties with the highest numbers of individuals below the poverty level are most needy.  Again, incorporate the data into your argument.

j) What other information might you seek in order to decide on fair way to distribute government assistance to the poor in Illinois?
