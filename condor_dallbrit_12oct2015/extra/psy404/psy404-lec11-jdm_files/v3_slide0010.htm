Framing Effects
Frame A:
1. Without the new
treatment, 700
soldiers will die
2. With the new
treatment, there is a
70% chance that all
of them will die, and
a 30% chance that
none will die.
Frame B:
1. Without the new
treatment, 300 will be
saved.
2. With the new
treatment, there is a
70% chance that none
of them will be saved,
and a 30% chance
that all of them will
be saved.
