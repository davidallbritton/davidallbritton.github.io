Conceptual Combination
• Modification?  (brown apple)
• Separate Prototypes? (big wooden spoon)
– But sometimes the combination has a prototypical
feature that is not typical of either noun individually
(pet birds live in cages, but neither pets nor birds do)
• Extending salient characteristics?
– When nouns are “alignable” (zebra horse)
– But non-alignable nouns are combined using a different
mechanism (zebra house)
