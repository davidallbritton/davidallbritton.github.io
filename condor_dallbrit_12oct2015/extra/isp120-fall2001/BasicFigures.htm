Some commonly accepted figures useful for making approximations

Population of the US in 1999: 270 million
Population of Chicago in 1990: 2.8 million
Average size of a household in the US: 2.7 people
Distance across the US: about 3000 miles
Circumference of the earth: about 25,000 miles
Height of highest mountain (Mt. Everest): about 29,000 ft.
Average distance to the moon: about 240,000 miles
Average distance to the sun: about 93 million miles
