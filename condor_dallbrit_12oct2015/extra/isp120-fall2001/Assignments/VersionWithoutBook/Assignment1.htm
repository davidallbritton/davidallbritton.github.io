ISP 120 Out of Class Assignment 1

Mathematical Autobiography

Due at the beginning of class, Wednesday, Jan. 10.

Task: Write a brief "mathematical autobiography" describing your lifetime experience, good and bad,with mathematics. Your narrative should include a summary of the mathematics and computer science courses you have had up to this point highlighting key courses or events. Be specific about which features were important to you both positively or negatively. Your narrative should also include your expectations for this course and what you think you need in the way of mathematics preparation for your future.

Purpose:

  1. for you to reflect on your experience with mathematics over your lifetime

  2. to encourage you to be more reflective about own learning

  3. for me, as instructor, to get to know you better

  4. to give you experience with a variety of writing genres in this course

Length: 1.5 to 2 double spaced type-written pages

Evaluation Criteria:

  1. Does the document give a coherent description of your experience?

  2. Does the document have a sufficient number of examples and details that support the main assertions?

  3. Is the document "reader friendly"-free of grammatical and formatting errors that distract the reader?

  4. Does the document  include your expectations for this course and what you think you may need in the way of mathematics preparation for your future?

Your grade is determined by how well you meet these criteria!
