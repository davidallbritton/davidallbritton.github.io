ISP 120

Activity 17: Prisoners in the US

Week 9

All group activities must include a signed statement that all members of your group participated fully in the assignment. To start you might want to open a new Word document entitled something like "Group Activity 16" and put the names of your group members at the top.

Objectives

1. Use the quantitative skills you have developed to report social data.

2. Learn how to use PowerPoint to make a simple presentation.

3. Gain experience with data presentation.

4. You will learn how to email a document.

Assignment

Open the file Prisoners.xls, which contains data on prisoners in the US, published by the Bureau of Justice Statistics in August of 1998; note that the file has five separate sheets of data.  You can navigate from sheet to sheet by clicking on the tabs at the bottom of the screen. Your task is to make a PowerPoint presentation summarizing this data.  Your presentation should have 5-7 slides.There should be a title slide with your group members' names.   Most of the slides should contain either a chart or a map. Note: in most cases you need to make calculations in Excel in order to put the data into "presentation shape", that is to make the data meaningful.  Also, choose your chart types carefully.  Remember that the purpose of presentations to communicate clearly and effectively.

Submission Process

Your PowerPoint presentation must be submitted via email to djabon@depaul.edu.
