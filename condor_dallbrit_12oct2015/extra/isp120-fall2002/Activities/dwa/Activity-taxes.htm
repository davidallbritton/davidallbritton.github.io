You will also need to refer to the 1040-EZ instructions for this assignment.  Please do NOT print out the 1040-EZ instructions!  It is over 20 pages long and that would be a tremendous waste of time and paper.  Instead, simply open the instructions file in your web browser and read them on the screen as needed.  You may print out this assignment page if you wish so that you do not have to read it and the EZ instructions on the screen at the same time.
