ISP 120

Activity 16:  PowerPoint Presentation

All group activities must include a signed statement that all members of your group participated fully in the assignment.

Objectives

1. Learn how to use PowerPoint to make a simple presentation.

2. Gain experience with data presentation.

Assignment

Open the file StateSATs.xls which contains data on average SAT scores and the percent taking the SAT in each state in the US.   Your task is to make a PowerPoint presentation summarizing this data.  Your presentation should have 4 slides. There should be a title slide with your group members' names and the title of your presentation.   The second slide should contain a scatterplot of the average SAT scores and the percent taking the SAT in each state along with a trendline and R-squared value.  You might want to add some text describing the issue. The third should show a map of the United States representing the percentage of students taking the SAT in each state.  The final slide should state the conclusions you can draw from the previous slide.  Remember that the purpose of presentations to communicate clearly and effectively.


Turn in a printed copy of your four slides.  (Upon clicking on "Print" you will find the option "Print what".  Choose to print "handouts" 2, 3, or 6 per sheet.  Printing the slides full size takes a long time and is somewhat wasteful for our needs.)
