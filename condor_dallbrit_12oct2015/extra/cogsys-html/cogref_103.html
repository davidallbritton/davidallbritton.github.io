3.3.5 TXTEXT

The TXTEXT creates pictures with text in them. Like the RANDEXT (see section RANDEXT), it uses subfunction numbers to identify the different functionalities the extension provides.

This is the syntax of the various forms of the TXTEXT:

Syntax: TXTEXT subnum [ arg1 ... ]
