3.3.4.5 RANDEXT Subfunction 5: grayscale square

Syntax: RANDEXT 5, graycolor

Draws a solid 64x64 gray square (of the specified graycolor shade of gray) centered on a light gray full picture (320x200) background.

The following example draws a dark gray square:

 
%X[randext.cxr]
%M2%C2
%I[1,5,3]
%B[1,20]
